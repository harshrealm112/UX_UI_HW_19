Alive at the Elephant: CD


by Jon Blondell


Nine great jazz tracks interpreted as only the monster, Jon Blondell, can do! This 9 track album, recorded in 2010 features Carter Arrington on guitar, Jeff Hellmer on piano, John Fremgen on bass, and J.J. Johnson on drums. To call this an album of "jazz standards" would be sorely remiss. Tracks like the Doors' "Riders On the Storm" and "In Walked Bo" are not that, but then again, "All the Things You Are" and "St. Thomas" are certainly standards but in no way "standard".


Who? CD


Jazzaholics


The Jazzaholics was the brainchild of composer/pianist Benjamin Irom. These original compositions by him, one by drummer Mike Koenning and another by bassist Thomas Machalek serve as a sample of the jazz creativity floating through the community of young, upcoming artists in 1997. Nine tracks recorded "directly to DAT" live from the Elephant


Live at the Elephant Room: CD


Various Austin Artists


The Monday Night Jazz Jam had become a true "hang" where t local jazz mainstays and the upcoming jazz students across central Texas came to mix it up. The result eatures, among others, the great Tony Campise, Dr. James Polk, Bob Meyer, Tomas Ramirez and Sandy Allen. This nine track CD features some favorite Jazz standards you know and love delivered by the cream of the crop of local artists in 1994.
